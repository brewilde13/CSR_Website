Global & National Initiatives

    For our Global Intiative, Alpha has selected SOS Children's Village as the Global Charity of the year. Our goal as an organization is to raise $20,000.00 on behalf of the organization. SOS impacts the lives of vulnerable children and families across in over 570 villages and 135 countries and terroritories through family support and care programs, medical centers, schools and emergency relief efforts. More information and the opportunity to donate can be found in the website link above in the Fundraising section.



    For our National Initiative, Alpha will be participating in American Heart Association Heart Walk across 6 different cities! The New York team has already completed the Heart Walk and raised over $1,800.00. Denver, Boston, San Francisco, Chicago and LA are all up next and we are excited to see the outcomes!


Local Initiatives

    In an effort to get the local offices across the US more involved in philanthropic work, Alpha US has asked each local office to select an organization of their choice to volunteer for at least once this fiscal year. It is proving to be a great success and we have enjoyed seeing all of the different offices get involved in their own ways!



    Special shout out to the San Francisco team for serving their first breakfast in the 'Daily Free Meals Programs' through the GLIDE foundation. This program requires volunteers each day to fill the breakfast, prep, lunch and dinner shifts, 364 days a year. The SF team played a part in serving approximately 2,000 meals - way to go team!



    Another shout out to the New York team for kicking off the first formalized CSR effort with the non-profit organization: Junior Achievement. Alpha conducted a 7 course lesson plan on Friday mornings introductin the basics of entrepreneurship.
